SIAG/LA organizes conferences and supports smaller, less formal meetings as requested by the membership. The primary conference of the SIAG is the SIAM Conference on Applied Linear Algebra, which takes place every three years; the SIAG/LA Business Meeting is always held at this conference. The SIAG also organizes the International Summer School on Numerical Linear Algebra every three years.

Listed below are upcoming meetings likely to be of interest to SIAG/LA members and past meetings in which the SIAG was involved. A list of advertisements for the upcoming meetings is also available.
