The SIAM Activity Group on Linear Algebra organizes a summer school: The SIAG/LA International Summer School on Numerical Linear Algebra (ISSNLA). The last meeting was in 2017 in Berlin, Germany.

The main goal of the SIAG/LA-ISSNLA is to offer accessible courses on current developments in Numerical Linear Algebra and on the applications of Numerical Linear Algebra to other disciplines. The SIAG/LA-ISSNLA is mainly intended for graduate students in any field where methods and algorithms of Numerical Linear Algebra are used.
