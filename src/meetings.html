The SIAM Activity Group on Linear Algebra organizes conferences and supports smaller, less formal meetings as requested by the membership.

The SIAM Conference on Applied Linear Algebra takes place every three years. The last meeting was held in June 2012 in Valencia, Spain. The next meeting will be held in Atlanta, Georgia, October 26 - 30, 2015.

The SIAM Activity Group on Linear Algebra Business Meeting is always held at the SIAM Conference on Linear Algebra.

The International Summer School on Numerical Linear Algebra takes place every three years (from 2010). The last meeting was in July 2013 at Fudan Unifersity, Shanghai, China.

A list of recent and upcoming meetings that may be of interest to members of the Activity Group is available.

Details about other meetings that have been sponsored by the Activity Group are available.

International Summer School on Numerical Linear Algebra

The SIAM Activity Group on Linear Algebra organizes a summer school: The SIAG/LA International Summer School on Numerical Linear Algebra (ISSNLA).

The main goal of the SIAG/LA-ISSNLA is to offer accessible courses on current developments in Numerical Linear Algebra and on the applications of Numerical Linear Algebra to other disciplines. The SIAG/LA-ISSNLA is mainly intended for graduate students in any field where methods and algorithms of Numerical Linear Algebra are used.
