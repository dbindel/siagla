The SIAM Activity Group on Linear Algebra organizes conferences and supports smaller, less formal meetings as requested by the membership. The SIAM Conference on Applied Linear Algebra takes place every three years. The last meeting was held in June 2012 in Valencia, Spain. The next meeting will be held in Atlanta, Georgia, October 26 - 30, 2015. The SIAM Activity Group on Linear Algebra Business Meeting is always held at the SIAM Conference on Linear Algebra. The International Summer School on Numerical Linear Algebra takes place every three years (from 2010). The last meeting was in July 2013 at Fudan Unifersity, Shanghai, China. We maintain information on recent and upcoming meetings that may be of interest to the members of the activity group; an index of these meetings and other meetings that have been sponsored by the Activity Group is provided below.
